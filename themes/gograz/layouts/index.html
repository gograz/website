Welcome to GoGraz

GoGraz is a user group for the Go programming language in Graz. We meet every 2nd Monday of the month usually at TAO Digital to discuss various topics related to Go and other relevant areas. No registration required! Just come and have fun!

You can also find us on Meetup.com, Twitter, and Mastodon!
